BLUE LAGOON

Blue Lagoon is a name for geothermal spa. It is the one of the most visited attracitons in Iceland.

The spa is located on a lava field Grindavík. Is is situated 39 km from the capital city Reykjavík, that is 50 minut by a car.

In 1981, people started bathing in it after its supposed healing powers were popularized. In 1992, the Blue Lagoon company was established,

and the bathing facility was opened for the public.
